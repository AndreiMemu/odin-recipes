Classic Chicken Parmesan

Description

This iconic Italian-American dish is the ultimate comfort food, celebrated for its satisfying harmony of bold flavors and contrasting textures. It features tender, juicy chicken cutlets encased in a perfectly seasoned, golden-brown Parmesan crust. The crispy exterior is topped with a vibrant, garlicky marinara sauce and a generous "crown" of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Every bite delivers a perfect balance of savory crunch, lively tomato tang, and rich, stretchy cheese.
