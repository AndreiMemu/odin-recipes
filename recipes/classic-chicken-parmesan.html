Classic Chicken Parmesan

Description

This iconic Italian-American dish is the ultimate comfort food, celebrated for its satisfying harmony of bold flavors and contrasting textures. It features tender, juicy chicken cutlets encased in a perfectly seasoned, golden-brown Parmesan crust. The crispy exterior is topped with a vibrant, garlicky marinara sauce and a generous "crown" of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Every bite delivers a perfect balance of savory crunch, lively tomato tang, and rich, stretchy cheese.

Ingredients:

  • The Chicken & Prep

    • 2-4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (pounded thin)
    • Salt, Black Pepper & Garlic Powder

  • The Breading Station

    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 2-3 large eggs (beaten)
    • 1 ½ cups Italian breadcrumbs (or Panko)
    • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • The Topping & Finish

    • 2 cups marinara sauce
    • 1-2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Fresh basil
    • Olive oil (for frying)

Steps:

  1. Prep and Bread the Chicken

    • Seasoning: Generously season both sides of your pounded chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
    • Dredging: Coat each piece in flour (shake off the exces), dip into the beaten eggs, and then press firmly omtp the breadcrumn-parmesan mixture until fully coated.
